Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Does the Curral app require internet to work?

A: No! Curral is designed to work 100% offline. All your data is securely stored directly on your iPhone or iPad.

Q: How do I add a new animal to my herd?

A: In the "Animals" tab, tap the "+" button at the top right corner of the screen and fill in the animal's information.

Q: Can I export data or reports from the app?

A: Yes! You can generate a "Field Sheet" in PDF from the "Producer" tab and "Graph Reports" in PDF from the "Graphs" tab, and then share them as you wish.
